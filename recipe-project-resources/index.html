Rice and Fried Stew (Ata Didin) Recipe

Yield: serves 5 people

INGREDIENTS:

Rice (5 cups)

Bell Pepper(Ata Rodo)

Raw Tomatoes

Salt

4 Seasoning Cubes

Smoked Fish

Vegetable Oil

2 Onion bulbs

Hand/Manual Blender

...and of course water...lol

Method: For Rice

    Preparations

  1. Measure 5 cups of rice in a bowl and rinse well with water

    2. ***You can also choose to parboil the rice for 5 to 10mins before you rinse it.

  2. Pour the rice in a clean pot of water and add salt (to taste).
  3. Cover the pot with a lid
  4. Cook the rice till its soft and dry.

    5. ***Your end product should look similar to the image in this section

Method: For Fried Stew (Ata Didin)

    Preparations

  1. Use the manual blender to blend the tomatoes, the pepper and 1 onion
  2. Slice the second onion to make onion rings as seen in the image.
  3. Pieces the smoked fish and rinse the pieces into a bowl

Now let's get cooking...

    Preparations

    I'm sure you know you have to light your gas *winks*

  1. Pour the vegetable oil in a clean pot
  2. Pour the onion rings into the vegetable oil and let it fry for a minute
  3. After which you add the blended pepper (tomato paste mix)
  4. Then add a little bit of water to the stew
  5. Then add salt(to taste), the seasoning cubes and the smoked fishs.
  6. Stir a little and let it fry for about 15 minutes before turning off the gas

Serving

Serve the rice and add the stew on it. Feel free to add beef, chicken turkey or ponmo (anyway you like it). Having a chilled cup or bottle of soft drink or juice isn't a bad idea either.
